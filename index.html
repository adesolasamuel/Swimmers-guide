  • Safety First even on the Beaches!!!

    All the time you see people swimming on the beach, in some scenarios due to lack of forehand knowledge, people go into the deep of the sea by mistake and are put in danger of drowning

  • Going for a swim on the beach

    This project is inspired to help swimmers and parents determine the depth of the swimming area before diving into the water. It measure the depth of water in the swimming area of the beach.

  • Swimmer's guide is a device that ensures your safety

    It is a hardware device that can be used to measure the depth of water in the swimming area of the beach or swimming pool to make a forehand decision of whether it is safe for swimming or not.
